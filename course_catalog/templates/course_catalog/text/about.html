About Qcumber

The Simple (Unofficial) Course Catalog For Queen's Students

Qcumber was made with passion by a Queen's undergraduate student named Chris, with the help and support of his friends, and at the prompting of one Nissi. The team has now expanded to include the awesome Carey and Phil.

We believe it will help to fill the severe lack of course-finding tools at Queen's University, specifically by providing a more modern way to browse courses and sections.

In the days, weeks, and months to come, it will will be updated to include building your timetable, sharing them with friends, rating courses, and much more. The exact set of features that will be added is also up to you! You can suggest or vote for features here.

If you'd like to help out in any way, please drop us a line at team@qcumber.ca to let us know! :)

P.S. Qcumber is open-source! You can check it out on GitHub at https://github.com/ChrisCooper/QcumberD.
