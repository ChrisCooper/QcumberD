About Qcumber

The Simple (Unofficial) Course Catalog For Queen's Students

Qcumber was made with passion by a Queen's undergraduate student named Chris, with the help and support of his friends, and at the nudging of one Nissi. The team has now expanded to include the awesome Carey and Phil.

We believe it will help to fill the severe lack of course-finding tools at Queen's University, specifically by providing a more modern way to browse courses and sections.

If you'd like to help out in any way, please drop us a line at team@qcumber.ca to let us know! :) Development at Qcumber has slowed over the past few months, so we're looking for helpers.

P.S. Qcumber is open-source! You can check it out on GitHub at https://github.com/ChrisCooper/QcumberD.
